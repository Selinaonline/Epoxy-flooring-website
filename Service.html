Our services include

Self Leveling Epoxy Flooring

Our Self-Leveling Epoxy is a premium two-component, 100% solids resin system engineered to deliver a flawless, seamless, high-gloss surface. When mixed and applied, the formulation flows out evenly—requiring minimal tooling—and cures into a strong, durable, chemical- and abrasion-resistant finish.

Broadcast Epoxy Flooring

Broadcast Epoxy Flooring is a high-performance, multi-layer flooring system that combines durability, slip resistance, and aesthetic appeal. It is created by broadcasting decorative or quartz aggregates into a wet epoxy base coat, then sealing it with a clear or pigmented topcoat. The result is a tough, textured, and seamless surface—perfect for environments that demand both function and style.

Artistic Metallic Epoxy Flooring

Metallic Epoxy Flooring is a stunning, high-gloss resin system designed to create a luxurious, three-dimensional look that mimics flowing lava, clouds, or marble. It combines durability with unique visual depth, making it perfect for both residential and commercial interiors that need to stand out. Each metallic floor is one-of-a-kind — no two designs are ever the same.

Floor Expansion Joint Sealing

Protect your floors and extend their lifespan with our expert expansion joint sealing. Using high-quality epoxy and polyurethane, we fill and seal joints to prevent cracks, water damage, and uneven surfaces. Perfect for industrial, commercial, and high-traffic areas, our solutions ensure durability, safety, and a smooth, professional finish.

MultiCoat Epoxy Flooring

Transform your floors with our multicoat epoxy system, delivering unmatched durability, chemical resistance, and a flawless finish. Multiple epoxy layers ensure superior protection against heavy traffic, spills, and wear while giving your space a sleek, professional look. Ideal for industrial, commercial, and residential areas, our multicoat epoxy floors combine strength, longevity, and aesthetic appeal.
